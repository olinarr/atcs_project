In a theoretical free society, all individuals act voluntarily while also having the power and resources to fulfill their potential. Individuals in a free society find it safe to be unpopular [REF]. This can be elaborated in terms of freedom of speech - if people have a right to express their views without fear of arrest, imprisonment, or physical harm. 

In a free society, individuals would organize in voluntary associations, including free market and communist societies. In a free society, individuals would gain more prosperity, of non-aggression principle.
