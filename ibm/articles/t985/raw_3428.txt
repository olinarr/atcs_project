In a theoretical '''free society''', all individuals act voluntarily while also having the power and resources to fulfill their potential. Individuals in a free society find it safe to be unpopular. <ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.quotationspage.com/quote/1383.html|author=Adlai E. Stevenson|title=Speech in Detroit|date=1952-10-07|authorlink=Adlai Stevenson II}}</ref>  This can be elaborated in terms of [[freedom of speech]] - if people have a right to express their views without fear of arrest, imprisonment, or physical harm.  . 

In a free society, individuals would organize in voluntary associations, including [[free market]] and [[communist]] societies. In a free society, individuals would gain more prosperity, of [[non-aggression principle]].  .
